Welcome to the MBP!

  • keyboard_arrow_down

We are happy that you have chosen the MBP to manage and control your IoT devices!


Let's start to set up your IoT environment in only seven steps:

1. vpn_key Register RSA keys that can be used by the MBP to access your IoT devices via SSH. (optional)

2. devices Register the IoT devices of your environment.

3. description Create extraction/control operators for your your sensors and actuators. Control operators are optional and only required if there are actuators in your environment.

4. settings_remote Register sensors and settings_remote actuators that are connected to your devices, by selecting a target device and an operator.

5. file_download Install operators on your sensors and actuators.

6. play_arrow Start your sensors and actuators.

7. call_split Define rules that make your environment smart and allow it to react to certain situations .


As an alternative, you can also use the view_quiltenvironment modelling tool to set up your environment at once.
