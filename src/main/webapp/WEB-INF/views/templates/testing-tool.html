Welcome!

Through this Testing-Tool you have the possibility to test your IoT-Applications!

Follow the given steps to create different tests:

    Step 1 - Register the extraction/control operators for the simulation of the Sensors and Actuators.

    Step 2 - Register the Testing Device, Testing Actuator and the necessary Sensor-Simulators you need for the test of your IoT-Application.

    Step 3 - Define the rules that are necessary for the your IoT-Application.

    Step 4 - Register the new test.

    Step 5 - Start the test and wait until the simulation is finished.

    Step 6 - Download the test report to find out the result of the test.
